Submitting an Archive

Do you have an archive that highlights a U.S. Veteran? Anyone is welcome to submit a request to add a Veteran's Archive onto the Cameron University Veteran's Project Database. Just send an email with the subject "CU Veteran's Project Submission Request" to compandtech@cameron.edu detailing the information of your veteran, interviewer, any accompanying media, and other documentations. You may also click the email linked below under "Contact". If approved, the archive will be added onto the database.
