Welcome to the Cameron University Library Combat Veterans Historical Site

This site is about interviews with Southwest Oklahoma Area veterans about their combat experiences in various theater of conflicts and wars.

Their Story

The audio interviews with the veterans about their combat experiences show how they defended our country by showcasing American values of bravery, duty, honesty, and integrity. The listener will gain a deeper understanding of the what, why, and how every veteran has sacrificed to successfully complete the missions given to them by every U.S. President, the Commander-In-Chief.

The site hopes to present to the general public these in-depth profiles of these heroes concerning their accomplishments, struggles, and obstacles they've overcome in order to protect our democracy.
