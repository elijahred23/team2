WELCOME

The Cameron University Veterans History Project site has collected interviews from combat veterans living Southwest Oklahoma area. The site's purpose is to preserve, and make accessible, the personal accounts of these veterans so that it is possible for future generations may hear directly from the veterans in order to have a deeper understanding of the realities of war.

Their Story

The site contains the audio interviews with the veterans about their combat experiences.

The site hopes to present to the general public, these profiles of these heroes concerning their accomplishments, struggles, and obstacles they've overcome in order to protect our democracy.
